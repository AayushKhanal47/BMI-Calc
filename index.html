About BMI

Understanding Body Mass Index and its limitations

What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a value derived from the weight and height of a person. It is defined as the body mass divided by the square of the body height, and is universally expressed in units of kg/mÂ², resulting from mass in kilograms and height in meters.

Limitations

BMI is a simple screening tool, but it has limitations. It doesn't account for factors like:

  • Muscle mass (athletes may have a high BMI but low body fat)
  • Age and sex differences
  • Body fat distribution
  • Ethnic and racial differences

Always consult with healthcare professionals for a comprehensive health assessment.
